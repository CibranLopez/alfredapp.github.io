For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: hello@alfredapp.com

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the Alfred service.

2.1 Data you provide directly

Data Purpose
Username Account authentication
Password (hashed, never stored in plain text) Account authentication
Company / business name Associating your account with your organisation
Contact email address Account recovery, legal notices

2.2 Data collected automatically

Data Purpose
Authentication tokens (JWT, stored in device secure storage) Keeping you signed in between sessions
Device platform (iOS / Android) Bug diagnosis and platform-specific support
App version Bug diagnosis and compatibility checks
Error logs (anonymised stack traces) Identifying and fixing crashes

2.3 Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect precise GPS location.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, or contacts unless you explicitly use a feature that requires it.
  • We do not collect advertising identifiers (IDFA, GAID).
  • We do not sell your data to third parties. Ever.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data listed in §2 solely to:

  1. Authenticate your identity and authorise access to your organisation's data.
  2. Display your business's operational data within the app.
  3. Diagnose and fix technical problems.
  4. Comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where data is stored

Your data is stored on servers provided by Render.com, located in the European Union / United States.

4.2 Security measures

  • Passwords are hashed using a strong one-way algorithm before storage.
  • Authentication tokens use HS256-signed JWTs with a short expiry (15 minutes in production).
  • All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Authentication tokens on your device are stored in the operating system's secure storage via flutter_secure_storage.
  • Database credentials are stored as environment variables, never in source code.

4.3 Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we will purge your personal data within 30 days.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

We share data only with:

Recipient Reason Data shared
Render.com (hosting) Runs the Alfred backend servers All app data as part of server operation
Your organisation's account administrator They manage your account within Alfred Your username, role, and activity within their company

No other third parties receive your data.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to certain processing activities.

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@alfredapp.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Alfred is a native mobile application. We do not use browser cookies.

We do not use third-party analytics SDKs. Error logs are collected through our own backend only.

8. Children's Privacy

Alfred is a business-to-business application intended for use by employees and managers of food-service businesses. It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
