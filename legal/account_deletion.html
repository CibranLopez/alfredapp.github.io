Account & Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At Alfred, we respect your right to privacy and your right to delete your personal account data. Because Alfred is an enterprise resource planning (ERP) and Point of Sale (POS) system for the hospitality industry, the deletion process handles your personal login data and business operational data differently.

How to delete your account inside the app

If you currently have the app installed, you can delete your account instantly by following these steps:

  1. Open the Alfred app and log in to your account.
  2. Tap your profile icon (the Alfred logo) in the upper right corner of the screen.
  3. In the menu that appears, tap the "Remove Account" symbol located in the bottom left corner.
  4. Confirm your decision. Your personal access credentials will be immediately revoked and deleted.

How to request account deletion via the web

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request the deletion of your account by sending an email to appalfred2026@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Please email us from the address associated with your Alfred account so we can verify your identity.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, we immediately and permanently delete all your personal login credentials. This includes your passwords, biometric authentication links, and active session tokens. You will no longer be able to log into the app.

What data is retained

Because Alfred is a business management system used by your employer/restaurant, operational records generated by you during your employment cannot be deleted. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Orders processed or taken at tables.
  • Inventory, receipts, or escandallos you created or modified.
  • Shift history and logged hours.

This operational data is retained securely by the restaurant manager/owner as it is legally required for accounting, tax auditing, and historical business records. Your name may remain attached to these historical documents solely for these compliance purposes.
